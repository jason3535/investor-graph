Born 1969 Hubei. Wuhan Univ CS. Kingsoft CEO → UCWeb → Xiaomi 2010 → Shunwei Capital 2011. Created the Xiaomi ecosystem model: invested in Dreame, Roborock, Narwal, Ninebot, Aqara, Loona/KEYi, hundreds more. Spawned massive hardware entrepreneurship wave. By 2026 Xiaomi's EV arm scaled fast: the YU7 SUV family expanded to 5 variants (standard ¥235.5K, GT ¥389.9K+), with 232K YU7s delivered Jul 2025–Apr 2026 (vs Model Y's 358K). Q1 2026 adjusted net profit was ¥6.1B even as the auto unit still ran a quarterly loss; the board launched a buyback of up to HK$20B. Xiaomi spent ¥105.5B on R&D over the past five years and plans >¥200B more, including ¥60B on AI over three years.
