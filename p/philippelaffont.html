MIT 计算机科学毕业；前老虎基金分析师（小虎）。1999 年创立 Coatue Management，科技为主的公私混合基金，资产规模约 400-700 亿美元。2025 年 Coatue 参与 16 笔独角兽融资，多于任何对冲基金，并围绕芯片、电力、数据中心搭建 AI 物理层。Coatue 领投 Sunday Robotics 1.65 亿美元 B 轮（2026）做自主家用机器人，联合创始人 Thomas Laffont 进入董事会。
MIT computer science graduate; former Tiger Management analyst ('Tiger Cub'). Founded Coatue Management in 1999,a tech-focused hybrid public-private firm with roughly $40-70B in assets. In 2025 Coatue participated in 16 unicorn financings — more than any other hedge fund — and built out the AI 'physical layer' across chips,power and data centers. Coatue led Sunday Robotics' $165M Series B (2026) for autonomous home robots,with co-founder Thomas Laffont joining the board.
